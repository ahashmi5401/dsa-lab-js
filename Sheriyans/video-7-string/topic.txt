### **Strings (Video 7) - Short Notes**

#### **1. Substring vs Slice**
* **`slice(start, end)`**: Best hai. Negative index (piche se) allow karta hai.
* **`substring(start, end)`**: Negative ko `0` Maan leta hai. Agar `start > end` ho toh swap kar deta hai.

#### **2. Reverse String**
* **Easy:** `str.split('').reverse().join('')` (Fast coding).
* **Efficient:** **Two-Pointer** (Left aur Right swap).
    * *Note:* String immutable hai, isliye pehle `split('')` karke array banana padta hai.


#### **3. Frequency Array (Counting)**
* `new Array(128).fill(0)` banao.
* `str.charCodeAt(i)` ko **index** maano aur `+1` karte jao.
* $O(n)$ time mein characters count ho jate hain.

#### **4. Case Toggle Trick**
* `char ^ 32` (Bitwise XOR).
* Uppercase $\leftrightarrow$ Lowercase flip karne ka sabse fast tareeka.
